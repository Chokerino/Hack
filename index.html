A very simple yet interesting games. You are given a bunch of question of various types ranging from audio to video and your only task is to recognize who was behind that great piece of entertainment.
You will only have two options to choose from making you task even easier. Every correct answer gives you 5 pts but every incorrect answer deducts 1 pt.
